About Us

Who We Are

The “Out of Last Place Alliance” is a partnership of business leaders dedicated to getting Mississippi permanently out of last place, as measured by the economic prosperity and wellbeing of our residents.

Our Vision

We want Mississippi to be a place where “last place” is permanently our “past place", so that no other state can legitimately still say “Thank God for Mississippi” Wikipedia.

Our Mission

To publish the most comprehensive research and information ever undertaken to definitively determine:

  • When and how Mississippi got in last place.
  • Why Mississippi is still currently, and has remained, in last place.
  • What specific, concrete, and actionable steps would be required to get Mississippi out of last place?
