OFFICIAL RULES GOVERNING RAFL INSTAGRAM GIVEAWAYS

By entering into a rafl giveaway, you acknowledge that you have read and understand these Official Rules, and that you agree to them.

IMPORTANT THINGS TO KNOW:
  • Liking a post DOES NOT enter you into the giveaway or give you bonus entries!
  • Each giveaway will have a cut-off date and time (likely 1-2 business days before the winner is announced). Any entries after the cut-off time won’t be added to the giveaway. Don’t worry, we’ll post and let you know what the cut-off day and time is!
  • Most gift cards in each giveaway are in-store only. In some cases, there may be gift card that can be used online, but most of the gift cards will not support online usage. Be sure you live in the area so you can use all the gift cards if you win!
  • Rafl is new. And like many new things, sometimes we may have to work some kinks out to make it better and better for you. If at any time you have a question, suggestion, or complaint, please emails us at info@raflgiveaways.com!
NO ENTRY FEE. NO PURCHASE OR OBLIGATION NECESSARY TO PLAY OR WIN. VOID WHERE PROHIBITED.

Details: rafl giveaways are held on a monthly basis in each city we work in. Gift cards are gathered from local businesses and there is ONE grand prize winner every month! Grand prize winners will receive all the gift cards from stores participating in that respective month’s giveaway.

To enter: You MUST follow ALL stores on Instagram participating in the current month’s giveaway. Note that following participating businesses does not enter you into the giveaway, but only makes you eligible to enter. Find “Entry Posts” on the rafl Instagram of your city and tag a friend (@mention them) in a comment. Entry Posts will be very plainly recognized as Entry Posts, and for additional clarity the words “ENTRY POST” will be included in the caption of each Entry Post. Entry Posts will be posted periodically throughout the month (once per week, per participating business, and once per week from your local rafl Instagram account). Limit one entry per Entry Post. Incomplete, unreadable, or unintelligible entries will be disqualified. Entrants who attempt to “spam” usernames in the Comments section on any Entry Post will be disqualified. All entries must be received by the specific deadline we share on our Instagram account for your city in the days leading up to it.

Bonus entries: In addition earning one entry on each of the Entry Posts found on the rafl Instagram account for your city, earn additional entries by finding Entry Posts on the Instagram feeds of participating stores. Each store participating in the current month’s giveaway will periodically post Entry Posts throughout the month. Tag a friend (@mention them) in a comment on each Entry Post you find in order to earn a bonus entry. Limit one entry per Entry Post. We will keep a running list of participating stores, which we will share periodically on our Instagram account for your city. This list is also readily available at http://www.raflgiveaways.com/giveaways-this-month.html.

Deadline: Entry deadline is always the 2nd business day before the first of the following month (ex. If the first of next month is Friday, July 1, the entry deadline will be on Wednesday, June 29). The specific deadline time for your city’s giveaway will be made publicly available on the rafl Instagram account for your city.

To enter by email without obligation: Email info@raflgiveaways.com. Please be sure to include the city you wish to enter into in the subject line. All email entries must be received by same deadline as mentioned above. Include your Instagram handle in the body of the email. Entrants wishing to participate via email are entitled to one entry per city, per month. No bonus entries will be granted via email.

Prizes and Odds: The prize in each city will be as follows: A collection of all the gift cards from participating stores within each city from each participating store for the current month’s giveaway. The value of each prize varies from month to month depending on the amount of participating stores. The odds of winning a prize depend on the total number of eligible entries received.

Eligibility: You MUST follow ALL stores on Instagram participating in the current month’s giveaway in order to be eligible to win. rafl Giveaways are open to legal residents of the state of Mississippi age 16 or older, who have an active Instagram account and Internet access as of the beginning of the promotion/contest period. Employees of rafl Giveaways, LLC and each participating store and their respective parent companies, affiliates, subsidiaries, advertising, promotion, and fulfillment agencies, their immediate family members and persons living in their same household, are not eligible to participate in the promotion/contest. Participation in the promotion/contest constitutes entrant’s full and unconditional agreement to and acceptance of these Official Rules & Regulations ("Official Rules"). VOID WHERE PROHIBITED. Any use of robotic, automatic, programmed or the like entry methods will void all such entries by such methods.

Per Instagram rules, we must mention rafl giveaways are in no way sponsored, administered, or associated with Instagram, Inc. By entering, entrants confirm they are 16+ years of age, release Instagram of responsibility, and agree to Instagram's term of use.

Winners are solely responsible for any and all federal, state, provincial and local taxes that apply to prizes. No cash or other substitution of prizes is permitted. Prizes won by minors will be awarded to the parents or legal guardians, who must sign all required affidavits and releases. rafl Giveways and participating businesses will not replace any lost or stolen prizes.

Notification of Winners: Winners will be notified via Direct Message on Instagram within three (3) business days of the prize drawing, and and may be required to sign and return an affidavit of eligibility and publicity/liability release within seven (7) days of notification. If we do not hear from the winner within 24 hours after attempting to contact them, we will select a new winner. If a selected Winner cannot be contacted, is ineligible, fails to claim a prize and/or where applicable an affidavit of eligibility and publicity/liability release is not timely received, is incomplete or modified, prize may be forfeited and an alternate Winner will be selected from remaining valid, eligible entries timely submitted. In the event of a dispute regarding who submitted an entry, the entry will be deemed submitted by the authorized account holder of the Instagram account specified in the entry. "Authorized account holder" is defined as the natural person who is assigned to an e-mail address by an Internet access provider, online service provider, or other organization (e.g., business, educational institution, etc.) that is used for the Winner’s Instagram account. No automatically generated entries will be accepted.
