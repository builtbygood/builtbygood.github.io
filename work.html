Wirki

UI/UX Design, Branding, Development

You can think of Wirki as a Wikipedia for your work where you choose the authors. With Wirki you can invite your peers to describe your work, rate your skills, and help you build your professional network. You choose who you want to contribute to your profile and editing is anonymous. This way, your profile is created by the people you trust and you get their honest assessment. And of course, you can edit your own profile too. We built Wirki using some of the latest technology available for building web applications, such as ReactJS & Redux on the front end and a Rails REST API on the back end.
