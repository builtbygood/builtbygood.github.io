We’re a group of business leaders dedicated to helping Mississippi win.

Our mission is to get our state permanently out of last place by answering hard questions with hard data to form real solutions.

So why are we in last place?

Personal income.

How big is the problem?
Bigger than you think.

In order to climb to 49th, Mississippi would need nearly 144,000 new jobs and total personal income would need to increase by $5 billion (4.5%). That means our per capita personal income needs to increase by $1,603 for every man, woman, and child in the state.

For Mississippi to get out of last place
we need to build a $5B economic bridge.

It won't be easy.

but that means there’s no where to go...
